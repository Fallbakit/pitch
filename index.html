01 The Problem

AI inference is a runaway cost.

Cloud bills scale with every token — and send your data outside your walls. Local hardware is cheaper and private, but breaks in production.

Cloud APIs
  • Reliable, easy to integrate
  • Cost scales with usage
  • Data leaves your network
vs
Your own hardware
  • Cheap, private, fast
  • Full control of data
  • Fragile & unmanaged in prod

Teams overpay for cloud — or hand-build routing, health checks, and fallback themselves.

02 The Solution

Serve AI from hardware you own.

One OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Fallbakit offloads production traffic to your local machines first, and falls back to cloud only when it must.

Secure outbound tunnel — no public ports, no app rewrite. Swap one base URL.
